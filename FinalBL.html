Depression: Causes, Symptoms, and Cures

What is Depression?

Depression is a mental health disorder that can affect your mood, thoughts, and behavior. It can cause feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and loss of interest in activities that you once enjoyed. Depression can also affect your physical health, causing symptoms such as fatigue, sleep disturbances, and changes in appetite or weight.

Causes of Depression

Depression can be caused by a combination of genetic, environmental, and psychological factors. Some common causes of depression include:

  • Family history of depression
  • Brain chemistry imbalances
  • Chronic illness or pain
  • Stressful life events, such as a divorce or job loss
  • Drug or alcohol abuse
  • Social isolation or lack of support

Symptoms of Depression

Depression can have a range of symptoms, which can vary from person to person. Some common symptoms of depression include:

  • Feeling sad or hopeless
  • Loss of interest in activities
  • Changes in appetite or weight
